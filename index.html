Heartbeat Terrain

A 3D terrain engine where vertex elevations are derived from Apple Watch heart rate and GPS. Perlin noise heightmaps computed on the GPU with Metal shaders, rendered in SceneKit. Building toward persistent landscapes that evolve with accumulated health data.
